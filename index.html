Available Services

One on One ADHD Coaching

Struggling with the challenges of ADHD can feel overwhelming, but you don't have to navigate it alone. I provide a supportive and fun environment where we can talk through your unique challenges, address internal saboteurs, find your strengths, and set meaningful goals tailored to your needs. Together, we'll develop strategies to enhance your daily life, improve productivity, and help you achieve your personal and professional aspirations.

In our one-on-one sessions, you'll receive individualized attention and guidance designed to empower you. Whether it's managing time, staying organized, overcoming procrastination, talking through rejection sensitivity dysphoria or imposter syndrome, and helping with emotional regulation, I'm here to support you every step of the way. Let's work together to empower your best self and create a life that aligns with your goals and values.

Couples ADHD Coaching

Neurodiverse relationships are wonderfully unique, filled with love, excitement, empathy, and surprises. However, like any relationship, they come with their own set of challenges. When one or both partners have ADHD, these challenges can become more complex, but with the right support, they are manageable. Couples ADHD coaching provides specialized assistance tailored to the distinct needs of relationships impacted by ADHD. Our coaching services are designed to foster understanding and develop effective strategies to manage ADHD symptoms. In our sessions, we will explore how certain behaviors are rooted in neurodiversity while focusing on achievable and sustainable growth. Whether one or both partners have ADHD, it is crucial to educate and validate without excusing behaviors solely due to ADHD. Let us strengthen your bond and deepen your understanding of each other, ensuring a more harmonious and fulfilling relationship.


Corporate ADHD Team Education


Approximately 1 in 7 adults in the UK have some kind of Neurodiversity. Different ways of thinking are an asset to any company, providing the potential for creative problem solving and innovation. Understanding your people can help you unlock their full potential. In today's fast-paced corporate world, inclusivity and understanding are key to fostering a productive and harmonious workplace.

I specialize in Corporate ADHD Inclusivity and Education, providing essential support and training to help organizations create an environment where employees with ADHD can be supported to thrive.

These services can include; ADHD Awareness workshops, Inclusive Strategies, Manager and Leadership Training, and ongoing support consultation.

Welcome

"Hello! My name is Angeline (Angel) Newman and I found my passion in ADHD Coaching! I am a dedicated and accredited ADHD coach with a degree in Psychology. My approach to coaching is centered around helping individuals with ADHD discover their unique strengths and develop effective coping mechanisms. I provide a supportive environment where we can talk through challenges, address internal saboteurs, and set meaningful goals.

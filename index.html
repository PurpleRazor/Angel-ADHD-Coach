FAQ'S

What is ADHD Coaching?
ADHD Coaching is a personalized one-on-one form of life coaching designed to support individuals with ADHD in navigating their unique challenges. Coaching is meant to help you gain to tools, resilience, and confidence to not need me as a coach anymore. Our one-on-one coaching sessions are tailored to address key areas such as organization, rejection sensitivity, imposter syndrome, impulse control, emotional regulation, your strengths, your saboteurs and more.

How often do I need to do sessions?
You can do sessions as often as you like, I have clients who are weekly, bi-weekly and even monthly. Sessions can be 30 minutes or an hour (As we know some ADHD'ers, can't sit for an hour). Clients typically do 8 to 12 sessions with me. What do we do in sessions? In coaching sessions, we will cover what YOU want to as every person is different. We can learn more about ADHD and how it affects you, your brain and body and those around you. We will focus on the areas you want to focus on and celebrate your wins and goals! In sessions we will start with a Win of the Week, go over how your goals from past sessions went, talk about what your week looked like and where you are struggling, then set new goals for the upcoming session.

What are your qualifications?
I have a Bachelors degree of honours in Psychology from the University of Kent and completed an ADHD Coaching course with ADHDWorks, taught by Leanne Maskel, the renowned author, creator, speaker and ADHD advocate. I have ADHD myself, but am also surrounded by ADHD'ers such as my husband, mother, siblings, and friends all who have ADHD in many different ways. I feel this gives me the ability to see that ADHD is not a "one size fits all". Every person is different and what helps one may not help all. This is why it is important to find what helps YOU.

Where do you coach?
I coach from home, so all my sessions are remote via Zoom and I coach people all over the world, though I am currently in England so I'm on the "BST Time Zone" working from 9am to 5pm.

Available Services

One on One ADHD Coaching

Struggling with the challenges of ADHD can feel overwhelming, but you don't have to navigate it alone. I provide a supportive and fun environment where we can talk through your unique challenges, address internal saboteurs, find your strengths, and set meaningful goals tailored to your needs. Together, we'll develop strategies to enhance your daily life, improve productivity, and help you achieve your personal and professional aspirations.
In our one-on-one sessions, you'll receive individualized attention and guidance designed to empower you. Whether it's managing time, staying organized, overcoming procrastination, talking through rejection sensitivity dysphoria or imposter syndrome, and helping with emotional regulation, I'm here to support you every step of the way. Let's work together to empower your best self and create a life that aligns with your goals and values.

Couples ADHD Coaching

Neurodiverse relationships are wonderfully unique, filled with love, excitement, empathy, and surprises. However, like any relationship, they come with their own set of challenges. When one or both partners have ADHD, these challenges can become more complex, but with the right support, they are manageable. Couples ADHD coaching provides specialized assistance tailored to the distinct needs of relationships impacted by ADHD. Our coaching services are designed to foster understanding and develop effective strategies to manage ADHD symptoms. In our sessions, we'll explore how certain behaviors are rooted in neurodiversity and focus on achievable, sustainable growth. Whether one or both partners have ADHD, it is crucial to educate and validate without excusing behaviors solely due to ADHD. Let us strengthen your bond and deepen your understanding of each other, ensuring a more harmonious and fulfilling relationship.

ADHD Team Education

Approximately 1 in 7 adults in the UK have some kind of Neurodiversity. Different ways of thinking are an asset to any company, providing the potential for creative problem solving and innovation. Understanding your people can help you unlock their full potential. In today's fast-paced corporate world, inclusivity and understanding are key to fostering a productive and harmonious workplace.

I specialize in Corporate ADHD Inclusivity and Education, providing essential support and training to help organizations create an environment where employees with ADHD can be supported to thrive.
These services can include; ADHD Awareness workshops, Inclusive Strategies, Manager and Leadership Training, and ongoing support consultation, to help you make the most of your team while building a professional relationship rooted in empathy.
